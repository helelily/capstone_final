Diabetes Incidence and Food Access in the United States

The United States has the highest incidence of diabetes in the developed world.

The FDA Food Atlas contains over fifty variables describing food access, security, assistance, and demographic makeup by county.

We can pull the locations of all grocery stores in each county using Yelp, and estimate the relative popularity of each store by the number of reviews.

With these two data sets, I will try to determine which store chains serve the majority of people living with diabetes in the US.

There are both commercial and public health applications for this information. We can decide which stores would be the best choice for distributing diabetes products, or which areas need greater food diversity and specialty store options.
