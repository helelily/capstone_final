Helene L Sajer

Fall 2016 Fellow at The Data Incubator

Hello

My name is Helene Sajer and I am currently a Fellow at The Data Incubator. After completing my MS in Financial Math at The University of Chicago, I worked in a hedge fund in Minneapolis to help build the company’s first top-down systematic risk management system. My main responsibilities included writing some of the pricing models in C#, building reports for investors and internal clients, and managing our team’s junior analyst. The experience of working to institutionalize our risk management system helped me discover a passion for using analytics to shape strategic decisions. I recently left that role to move back to the East Coast and find a position that would focus on on analytics and data science in a business facing context. My other main interest besides mathematics is foreign languages and cultures. I studied Chinese and Spanish for several years and lived in China for a year during college. I really love the idea of observing culture through data, and then finding innovative business applications for that insight. That might mean looking at consumer purchase decisions and coming up with a new product idea, or looking at a company’s internal data to understand the behavior of the employees, and having that inform the organizational design.
